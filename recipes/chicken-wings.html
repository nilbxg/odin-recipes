Chicken Wings

Main Menu

Description

Crispy, crunchy, saucy. I don't what better food you could want than a chicken wing. My absolute favorite food to make. And if you have an air fryer, *chef's kiss*. This recipe is simple and quick, you'll be lickin' your fingers before you know it!

What We'll Need

  • 1 lb of chicken wings
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder

Directions

  1. Season chicken wings well in a bowl
  2. Place in air fryer and cook at 400 degrees for 20 min, flipping halfway
  3. Let them rest for a few mintues, toss in your favorite sauce, and you're good!

Easy right?? Now get in that kitchen and FEAST!
