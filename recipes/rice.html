White Rice

Description

Here's my holy grail of recipes, folks. I know everyone has struggled with making some sticky white rice. After years of trial-and-error, I finally have the perfect recipe. ALl you need is an instant pot, and you'll have the stickiest of the icky. Let's get into it.

Here's What We'll Need

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 cup of water

Directions

  1. Add your rice and water to your Instant Pot
  2. Have the pressure cook it for 3 min, then let rest for 10 min (don't take the lid off)

And that's that! The rice should come out perfect. Let me know what you think please!
